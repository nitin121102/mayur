Student Ambassador Program

Thank you for your interest in joining the Student Ambassador Program at TheRightDoctors, We are a Google Launchpad Product Startup in the Digital Health space. Here you will find more information about the program benefits, the requirements to become an Ambassador, and how to take your first step to apply to the program.

BENEFITS

  • Access and insights into new applications.
  • Opportunities to meet and interview Prominent Leaders of Medical Fields.
  • Be a medium for Publishing Research Papers on our Website from your College!
  • Access to the Ambassador Network.
  • Use of title of Ambassador for CV development.

REQUIREMENTS

  • You should belong to top 20 percentile of your college.
  • Know your college the best.
  • Inquisitive, Curious and have organisational skills

What will you DO?

  • Ideate on and execute innovative campaign plans.
  • Work on live marketing projects for the brand.
  • Strategise and drive Word of Mouth in campus events and activities
  • Set up and moderate a Facebook page for The Right Doctors on your campus
